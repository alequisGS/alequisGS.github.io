About

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at PUC-Rio, working under the supervision of Sergey Galkin. My research is centered around algebraic geometry, Homological Mirror Symmetry, derived categories, Fano and log del Pezzo surfaces, Landau-Ginzburg models, periods, monodromy, and arithmetic aspects of mirror symmetry.

Research interests

  • Homological Mirror Symmetry
  • Fano and log del Pezzo surfaces
  • Derived categories and exceptional collections
  • Landau-Ginzburg models and periods
  • Arithmetic geometry and monodromy
  • Weighted projective hypersurfaces

Current project

I am currently studying the degree-10 hypersurface X10 ⊂ P(1,2,3,5), a log del Pezzo surface of index one with a non-Du Val quotient singularity of type 1/3(1,1). I am interested in its birational geometry, minimal resolution, canonical Deligne-Mumford stack, exceptional collections, and possible mirror Landau-Ginzburg models.
